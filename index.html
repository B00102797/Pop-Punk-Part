The Pop Punk Party

The Pop Punk Party is a festival celebrating all things punk. From punk rock to steampunk, we love it all and it will all be there.

People of all backgrounds are welcome here! Because there's nothing more punk than ignoring segregation.

There will be many activities you can take part in such as:

  • Punk Fashion Show
  • Steam Or Cyber: Which is the better aesthetic debate?
  • How To Survive A Mosh Pit training
